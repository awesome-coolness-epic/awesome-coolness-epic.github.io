mblep - album
genre: electronic (i think? it's a little all over the place)
fully instrumental
released on 13/11/25 (DD/MM/YY)
number of songs - 14
includes bonus section
runtime - main section approx 28:08
bonus section approx 13:11
total approx 41:32
made in ultrabox

many many manyyy samples taken from kommit's sample repo (the whole site's pretty awesome and inspired me to make this one so i suggest you look around there)

my first album consisting of some of the first songs i've made that i'm proud of!

i've showed it around to people i know and everybody says it's soooo cool.

very inspired by the soundtracks of undertale, deltarune (+ their fantracks), terraria (+ calamity mod), and many other artists and general bits and bobs that've stood out to me.

overall i think it's pretty solid for my first album. it's all quirked up and radical and i like to listen to it whilst doing homework or chores or whatever. i do have a few gripes, (it's not really themed around anything, some of the songs are really short) but at least i've learned things and made a final product that me and others think is genuinely good.

and yeah i chose that release date so i could say "look for my album on november 13th" like the gummibär music video

maybe someday i could give it a remaster? like especially once i've learned fl studio. i'd definitely give it a name with an actual meaning then lol

the playable thingalings are in .mp3 and the file garden links are in .wav form.
